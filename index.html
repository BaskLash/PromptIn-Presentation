Our Vision

Revolutionizing AI Prompt Management

PromptIn started as a simple idea in the Hackergarten community and has evolved into a comprehensive solution for AI prompt management. Our goal is to create a central database for prompts that enables users to save time and achieve better results.

We believe that the best prompts shouldn't be lost or forgotten. Instead, they should be preserved, shared, and continuously improved by the community.

Join thousands of users who are already saving time and improving their AI interactions with PromptIn.

🌍 Environmental Responsibility

Artificial intelligence consumes significantly more energy than traditional computing. To contribute to environmental sustainability, it's crucial to provide AI systems with the best possible questions to get optimal answers without the need for endless back-and-forth questioning. When responses don't match expectations, energy gets wasted in an unreasonable way. PromptIn helps you get it right the first time, reducing unnecessary energy consumption and making your AI usage more environmentally responsible.
