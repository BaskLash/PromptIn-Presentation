Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We, the PromptIn Team, take the protection of your personal data very seriously and strictly comply with the rules of data protection law, in particular the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, process and use personal data on our website prompt-in.com.

2. Controller

The controller within the meaning of the GDPR is:
PromptIn Team

3. Collection and Processing of Data

3.1. Visiting the Website

When you visit our website, certain data is automatically collected by our hosting provider (Vercel Inc.) and stored in server log files. These data include:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Date and time of access
  • Type of browser and operating system used
  • Referrer URL (the previously visited website)
  • Pages and files accessed

These data are used solely to ensure the smooth operation of the website, for error analysis and to improve our offering. The data are deleted after 7 days unless there is a statutory retention obligation. The legal basis for this processing is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in the technical functionality of the website).

3.2. Contact

If you contact us on GitHub at PromptIn, we store the data you provide (e.g. name, e-mail address, content of the message) in order to process your inquiry. These data will be deleted as soon as they are no longer required for handling your request, unless statutory retention obligations exist. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures).

3.3. Chrome Extension

Our Chrome extension “PromptIn” does not process personal data unless you actively enter such data (e.g. in the form of prompts). These data are stored exclusively locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. More information about the data processing of the Chrome extension can be found in the privacy policy on the Chrome Web Store.

4. Disclosure of Data

Your personal data will not be passed on to third parties unless this is necessary to comply with legal obligations or you have expressly consented. Our hosting provider (Vercel Inc., 340 S Lemon Ave #4133, Walnut, CA 91789, USA) has access to the server log files mentioned above, but is contractually obliged to comply with the GDPR (order processing agreement in accordance with Art. 28 GDPR).

5. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. If we use cookies in the future, we will inform you in advance and obtain your consent in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR): Right to obtain information about the personal data processed.
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): Right to rectification of incorrect data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): Right to erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction (Art. 18 GDPR): Right to restriction of processing.
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR): Right to receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR): Right to object to the processing of your data, in particular on grounds relating to legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Complaint (Art. 77 GDPR): Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g. the competent data protection authority in your federal state).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at PromptIn.

7. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss or misuse. Our website uses an encrypted connection (HTTPS) via TLS to ensure the security of data transmission.

8. External Links

Our website contains links to external websites (e.g. GitHub, Chrome Web Store). We have no influence on the content and data protection practices of these websites and therefore assume no responsibility for them. Please consult the privacy policies of the respective providers.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to adapt this privacy policy in order to comply with changed legal requirements or changes to our services. The current version of the privacy policy is always available on this page.

10. Contact

For questions about data protection, please contact:
PromptIn Team
